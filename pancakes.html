Description

Light, fluffy, American-style pancakes perfect for honey, maple syrup or lemon.

Ingredients

  • 150g self-raising flour
  • 125ml milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a well in the centre, and add the milk and the vanilla extract.
  3. Mix the ingredients into a smooth batter using a whisk.
  4. Heat a frying pan on the stove.
  5. Add a ladle-full of the batter to the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles start to form on the top of the pancake.
  7. Flip and cook on the other side until golden.
